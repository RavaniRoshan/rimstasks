About the Event

InnovateX is MIT's flagship annual technology festival, bringing together 3000+ students, industry experts, and innovators for three days of workshops, hackathons, and inspiring talks.

Whether you're a coding prodigy, a design enthusiast, or just curious about emerging tech, there's something for everyone.
